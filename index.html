The Force

The concept of The Force is described as the energy that sorrounds everything. Every single piece of the whole Universe is related somehow to it. Certain entities (mostly creatures) are sensitive to The Force and can react to its influence, even by learning to control it. Some organizations based on The Force, like the Jedi and the Sith, tend to look for these special beings and train them. Manipuling The Force provides a huge (almost infinite) set of special skills to the one who can do it, like controlling objects without touch them, move faster, jump higher, trick others minds, create lightnighs and even search in the past and the future. Many Force sensitive creatures can sense how other beings feel and understand them without the need of a language. This includes animals.
